 If you are starting with VanillaDesk and you are not sure which parts of the application require your attention before using, we have prepared a initial configuration „TO-DO list“, that will put you on the right track.

 Even though you are able to use VanillaDesk right out of the box, there are some parts you would like to configure to meet your companys` individual needs.

To save you the trouble of rummaging through the configuration options, the most important parts have been placed into the TO-DO list.

Each entry of the list contains:

  • a short explanation of each setting and its importance
  • simple tutorial videos, that will guide you through setting up each list entry
  • link to lead you directly to the setting

{% news_img todo.png %}

 

Accessing the TO-DO list is easy:

  • it is the first screen you see, when you start using VanillaDesk
  • a smaller version will always be visible in the sidebar, reminding you of the settings that have not been configured yet

{% news_img todolist.png %}

The list will hide automatically, once you get through all recommended settings.
