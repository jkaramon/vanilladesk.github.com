--- published: true layout: post title: Suggestions summary: New in VanillaDesk - Suggestions! ---

It does not require too much thinking that resolved tickets and knowledge-base articles provide valuable source of information e.g. while solving another ticket. So we designed "suggestions".

How can Suggestions help you:

Anytime you look at the ticket (any type), system will try to analyze all existing tickets in the database and all existing knowledge articles and will try to list those, which might be relevant for your current issue.

We are aware of the fact, that the engine evaluating relevance is "not perfect yet", but we think you still may find it usefull.

Obviously, any ideas for improvement are welcome.
