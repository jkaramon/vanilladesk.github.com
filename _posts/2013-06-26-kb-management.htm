We announce a new addition in our latest product version, VanillaDesk Knowledge Base Management. As we designed this feature, we want to provide you a standardized way to resolve common incidents.

Our knowledge base should extend your working processes:

Close call with an existing article

It is possible to link a call to exiting KB article without creating an incident.

Create KB article from existing ticket

All problems and incidents can be converted into KB article using predefined template.

Operator suggestions

While working with ticket, the operator has a new suggestions sidebar at disposal. The suggested tickets and articles can help with ticket resolution or finding similarities.

Portal extensions

All published articles are available in the customer portal now. During ticket creation in portal, the end users get related KB articles suggestions which can be useful before ticket creation.
