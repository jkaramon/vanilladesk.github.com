--- published: true layout: post title: Knowledge Base Re-implemented summary: Re-implemented Knowledge Base has been re-introduced to VanillaDesk. ---

We announce a re-implementation of Knowledge Base (KB) in our latest product version.

This new simple but cool feature can help you as follow:

Suggestions

KB articles appear in the list of suggested tickets/articles which our system finds relevant for the ticket operator is working on.

Close a call by relating it to an existing article

It is possible to link a call to an exiting KB article without creating an incident. This can be e.g. used for know-errors or very often repeating issues.

Create KB article from an existing ticket

All problems and incidents can be converted into KB article using predefined template.

Portal extensions

All published articles are available in the customer portal too. End users will be provided with suggestions for KB articles which might be relevant for their issue while creating a ticket through the customer portal.
