VanillaDesk has just released a new version of its Service Desk software provided as a service. The new version comes with the Customer Portal, Task Management and improved Reporting capabilities (SLA).

VanillaDesk Customer Portal is a self-service portal allowing End-users to submit and review the status of their service requests.

VanillaDesk Task Management provides a basic functionality and is integrated with Incident, Request and Problem Management.
