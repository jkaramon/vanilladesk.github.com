It's easier now to see what is going on in VanillaDesk

We introduced new reporting capabilities to help you oversee the functioning of your service desk. Use the new SLA and worklog reports to improve its processes.

  •  SLA reports - allow tracking the availability of your services, mean time between failures, and any breaches to the agreed conditions.
  •  Worklog reports - evaluate the effort spent to resolve tickets, manage overtimes and more...

The interface has also been enhanced. Multiple outputs for each report, new filtering and grouping options are only some of the new features.
