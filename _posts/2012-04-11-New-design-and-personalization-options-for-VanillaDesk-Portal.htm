 We have updated the design and layout of VanillaDesk Portal – it has been made more intuitive and given a new, modern look. Also many personalization options were added, so you can apply your company web page coloring easily.

  {% news_img portalsettings.png %} Using the new options for personalizing VanillaDesk Portal, you can adjust its looks to match your corporate colors for a seamless integration into your web. 

To make the personalizing quicker, we also introduced an interactive preview of the Portal settings – to see your changes as soon as you make them.
